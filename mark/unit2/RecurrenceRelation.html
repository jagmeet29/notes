Recurrence Relation

It is an equation that recursively defines a sequence where the next term is a function of the previous terms.

Examples:

  1. $$s=\{2,2^2,2^3,....2^n\}$$ $$s= \{s_n\} = \{2_n\}$$

    Recurrence relation

    $$s_n=2s_n-1 \ , n>=2 \ , s_1=2$$
  2. $$s=\{3,7,11,15,19,....\}$$

    Recurrence relation

    $$s_n=s_{n-1}+4\ , n>=2\ , s_1=3$$
  3. $$s=\{1,1,2,3,5,7,13,....\}$$

    Recurrence relation

    $$s_n=s_{n-1}+s_{n-2} \ , n>=3 \ , s_1 = s_2 =1$$
