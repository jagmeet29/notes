Generative Function

Generative function is a formal power series in one or more variables. It is used to represent sequences and to study their properties. The generative function of a sequence $a_n$ is defined as: $$ \begin{align} \text{sequence: } & a_0,a_1,a_2,a_3,a_4,\ldots,a_n \\ G(x) &= a_0 + a_1x + a_2x^2 + \cdots + a_nx^n \\ &= \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} a_n x^n \end{align} $$ where $G(x)$ is the generative function, $x$ is a variable, and $a_n$ are the coefficients of the series.

Aspect Arithmetic Progression Geometric Progression
General Term Formula $T_n = a + (n-1)d$ $T_n = a \cdot r^{n-1}$
Sum of First $n$ Terms $S_n = \frac{n}{2}[2a + (n-1)d]$ or $S_n = \frac{n}{2}(a + \ell)$ $S_n = a\left(\frac{1 - r^n}{1 - r}\right)$ (for $r \neq 1$)
Infinite Series (if applicable) Not applicable $S_\infty = \frac{a}{1 - r}$ (if $r<1$)

Examples:

  1. Sequence: $1,1,1,1,\dots$ or $a_k=1$
    $G(x)=1x^0+1x^1+1x^2+1x^3+\dots=\frac{1}{1-x}$ for $|x|<1$
  2. Sequence: $2,2,2,2,\dots$ or $a_k=2$
    $G(x)=2x^0+2x^1+2x^2+2x^3+\dots=\frac{2}{1-x}$ for $|x|<1$
  3. Sequence: $-1,-1,-1,-1,\dots$ or $a_k=-1$
    $G(x)=-1x^0-1x^1-1x^2-1x^3+\dots=\frac{1}{1+x}$ for $|x|<1$
  4. Sequence: $0,1,2,3,\dots$ or $a_k=k$
    $G(x)=0x^0+1x^1+2x^2+3x^3+\dots=\frac{x}{(1-x)^2}$ for $|x|<1$
  5. Sequence: $0,0,1,1,1,\dots$
    $G(x)=0x^0+0x^1+1x^2+1x^3+\dots=\frac{x^2}{1-x}$ for $|x|<1$
  6. Sequence: $1,1,0,0,1,1,\dots$
    $G(x)=1x^0+1x^1+0x^2+0x^3+1x^4+1x^5+\dots=\frac{1}{1-x}-x^2$ for $|x|<1$
  7. Sequence: $3,-3,3,-3,3,-3,\dots$
    $G(x)=3x^0-3x^1+3x^2-3x^3+\dots=\frac{3}{1+x}$ for $|x|<1$
  8. Sequence: ${}^8C_0,\ ^8C_1,\ ^8C_2,\dots,\ ^8C_8,\ 0,\ 0,\dots$
    $G(x)={}^8C_0x^0+{}^8C_1x^1+{}^8C_2x^2+\dots+{}^8C_8x^8=(1+x)^8$
  9. $a_k=2+3k$
    $G(x)=\frac{2}{1-x}+\frac{3x}{(1-x)^2}$
  10. $a_k=4^k$
    $G(x)=1+4x+16x^2+\dots=\frac{1}{1-4x}$
  11. $a_k=(-2)^k$
    $G(x)=\frac{1}{1+2x}$

Important for Representing series in Generative Function

The following table summarizes the relationship between the sequence $a_k$ and its corresponding generative function $G(x)$:

S.No. $a_{k}$ $G(x)$
1 $1$ $\frac{1}{1-x}$
2 $(-1)^{k}$ $\frac{1}{1+x}$
3 $c$ $\frac{c}{1-x}$
4 $k+1$ $\frac{1}{(1-x)^{2}}$
5 $k$ $\frac{x}{(1-x)^{2}}$
6 $a^{k}$ $\frac{1}{1-ax}$
7 $k^{2}$ $\frac{x(1+x)}{(1-x)^{3}}$
8 $k.a^{k}$ $\frac{a.x}{(1-ax)^{2}}$
